Acoustic Mirror is a streaming concert and experimental broadcast series entitled after a device used to cast and focus sound over great distances. The series emulates this phenomena by creating a point of reflection for musicians and sound artists wherever sound may reach.

Over 4 episodes the series has brought together the artists Philippe Battikha, Tim Shaw, Esther Bourdages, Chienvoler, Kelly Ruth, Patrick Breiner, Gambletron, Steve Bates, and Luke Stewart. The archives can be streamed at www.acousticmirror.net.
