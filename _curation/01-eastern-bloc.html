From 2014-2018 Rodriguez worked as the Technical Director, Lab Director, and Co-Director of Eastern Bloc, an artist-run centre dedicated to presenting emerging media art, located in Montreal QC.

Some of the projects which he curated or co-curated include the international digital and media art festival Sight + Sound, the exhibition Amplification, the radio program and intervention series PirateBlocRadio, as well as the educational workshop programing for the Eastern Bloc Lab.
